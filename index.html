Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We're here to help and make things clear.

What type of clients do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from startups and SMEs to large enterprises across tech, finance, retail, healthcare, and more. We tailor our approach to every client's unique needs and goals.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A brand identity project typically takes 4–6 weeks, while a full web development engagement ranges from 8–16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

What makes your agency different?

Our agency blends creativity, data-driven strategy, and technology to deliver measurable results. Every project is approached with personalized attention to detail and a focus on long-term impact — not just deliverables, but real business outcomes.

Do you offer post-project support?

Absolutely. We offer ongoing support packages — from maintenance retainers to full growth partnerships. We believe in building long-term relationships well beyond launch.

How can we start working together?

Reach out via our contact form or email hello@innovexa.co. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your goals and propose the best path forward. No commitment required for the initial call.

Is my data secure with Innovexa?

Absolutely. We follow strict data security protocols including NDAs, encrypted communications, and limited access policies. Your business information is never shared with third parties.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we are happy to sign a non-disclosure agreement before any project discussion. Protecting your intellectual property and business ideas is our top priority.

What services do you specialize in?

We specialize in brand strategy, digital product design, performance marketing, web & app development, and data analytics. We offer end-to-end solutions tailored to your business goals.

Can you handle both design and development?

Yes — we are a full-service agency. Our team includes designers, developers, strategists, and marketers who collaborate under one roof to deliver cohesive, end-to-end digital products.

How does your pricing work?

Our pricing is project-based or retainer-based depending on your needs. We provide detailed proposals after the discovery call so you know exactly what you're investing in — no hidden fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, major credit cards, and digital payment platforms. Invoicing schedules are agreed upon at the start of each project — typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.

What are your business hours?

Our core office hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm WIB (Jakarta time). However, our team is spread across time zones, so we accommodate client calls at flexible hours upon request.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients globally and are experienced in cross-border collaboration. All project management and communication happens through shared tools and video calls.
